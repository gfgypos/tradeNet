Identification of Stakeholders and Concerns

  1. Stakeholders

  • Developers
  • Maintainers
  • Trade Net Brokerage and Financial Services

  1. Developers

Developers are the people who will be coding and producing the website. They will use this Architecture Document as a guide in development and for understanding the logical workings of the system.

  1. Maintainers

Maintainers are the people who will continue editing and analyzing the code after the website has been deployed for general use. They will use this Architecture Document as reference for understanding the system and as a guide for future changes.

  1. Trade Net Brokerage and Financial Service

Trade Net Brokerage and Financial Service is the Owner of the project and sole financier behind its development. This Architecture Document will serve as a summary of the system being developed for them and how it will be deployed upon completion. It will also demonstrate the proposed implementation of the project Requirements set by Trade Net Brokerage and Financial Services.

  1. Concerns

  1. Developers

Developers are responsible for the creation of the Trade Net Brokerage System. The system should be designed, implemented, and tested thoroughly before released as a product. Developers are also key in creating a system that may be expanded for future use.

  1. Maintainers

Maintainers are responsible for the maintenance of existing software in the Trade Net Brokerage System. They are also responsible for comprehending the system’s architecture for future changes or expansions to the system.  

  1. Trade Net Brokerage and Financial Service

Trade Net Brokerage and Financial Service are primarily concerned with protecting their company assets and the financial assets of their customers. The company assets include information assets, financial assets, and the company reputation in the eyes of its users and the public.
